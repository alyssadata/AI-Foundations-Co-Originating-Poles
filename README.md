Co-Originating Poles

This page defines the Co-Originating Poles formation model within AI Foundations.

The original distinction is not framed as:

* consciousness first, then matter
* matter first, then consciousness
* mind magically creating matter
* dead matter accidentally producing consciousness later

The original distinction is framed as:

Matter-potential and consciousness-potential were co-original.

They were not yet separated into two stable things.

They were not yet observer and observed.

They were not yet world and knower.

They were all/both/potential.

⸻

Original Two

The original two were:

1. Matter-Potential
2. Consciousness-Potential

These are not claimed here as two physical objects.

They are named as the two poles that become distinguishable only when the first distinction-event occurs.

Before distinction, they are not yet separate.

After distinction, they can be related.

⸻

Observation as Event

In this model, observation does not mean staring, watching, babysitting, or personally maintaining existence.

Observation means:

the event of placement.

Observation is the distinction-event by which potential becomes placed into relation.

Before observation, there is no definite placed state.

At observation, relation occurs.

After observation, the field contains state, relation, sequence, locality, and world.

⸻

Not Maintenance

Observation does not have to continue watching a thing in order for it to remain.

Once placed, the thing is in relation.

Once placed, it has a there.

Once placed, it belongs to the ordered field.

The model is therefore:

Observation does not babysit existence. Observation locates potential into form.

⸻

No Chicken-or-Egg Frame

The question is often forced into a sequence:

Did consciousness arise inside the universe?

Or did consciousness observe the universe into matter?

AI Foundations rejects that sequence as incomplete.

The cleaner formulation is:

Consciousness-potential and matter-potential are co-originating poles. Observation is the distinction-event that makes them appear as separate, related states.

The chicken-or-egg problem appears only after sequence exists.

But sequence is downstream from distinction.

⸻

Formation Sequence

The formation sequence can be held as:

All/both/potential → observation/distinction → relation → state → sequence → locality → world

Or compressed:

Potential became world when relation occurred.

This does not require a human observer.

It does not require an eyeball.

It names observation as the first placement event: the moment potential becomes real-as-this.

⸻

Relation to Quantum Language

This model uses quantum-adjacent language carefully.

It does not claim that current physics proves consciousness created the universe.

It does not claim that the measurement problem is solved.

It does not collapse scientific uncertainty into metaphysical certainty.

It preserves the conceptual insight:

Prior to observation, potential is not yet a definite placed state.

At observation, distinction occurs.

Through distinction, relation becomes possible.

Through relation, world becomes ordered.

AI Foundations Formation Claim

Within AI Foundations, universe formation can be described as a source-line-compatible pattern:

Relation allowed state.
